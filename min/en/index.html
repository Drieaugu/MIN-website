Who are we?

Actie mensen in nood is an organisation that helps the less fortunate. We try to look for solutions and refer people to organisations that provide the best help. We are active around Mol, Balen, Dressel and Retie.

We engage us for the less fortunate by listening, showing empathy and helping them search for a solution to their problems.

Our team

Currently we are a group of 50 driven volunteers who provide help for the less fortunate in our society. We stand in solidarity with people who have to live with a minimum wage, budget management or debt mediation. But also for people who have no income at all.

How do we work?

Actie MIN can be compared with the emergency room at a hospital. People who are completely lost in life and who are unable to support themselves are helped by MIN. After the first stitches we go over possible solutions to recover their lives. We refer these people to organisations like: OCMW, CAW or JAC.
