Donations

Donating food

Do you wish to donate food items like: fruit, vegetables, dairy and other non-perishable items to Actie MIN? Contact us via mail: actiemenseninnood@gmail.com or call us: 0473 24 13 61.

Foodbox

At Carrefour market, Statiestraat 53A in Mol you can find our ‘Foodbox’ where it is always possible to donate dried food items.

Sponsoring

Trooper

Are you a frequent online shopper? Than you can support us via Trooper.be. Buy goods online using our link and we receive a small percentage of the amount spend.

De Warmste Week

We are a 'De Warmste Week'-organisation and for the second year in a row various projects have helped us save up more money.
