Store

OP=OP

What?

We hand out food in our store 'OP=OP', this is a place where people who are unable to purchase sufficient provisions are able to make sure that they can provide healthy food for their family. The food is a way for people to get to us so we can help them in their search for a better life. Every week we make sure we can hand out plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables. On Tuesdays and Thursday we provide extra bread. Every week we help around 140 families and we have around 200 members who are eligible. Over 450 families have already been helped at MIN.

When and for who?

Food distribution – only for members. Everybody is supposed to sign up in the waiting room upstairs.

  • Every Saturday between 8:30 AM and noon.
  • Last registration at 11:30 AM

Bread distribution – only for members (entrance at the rear of the building)

  • Every Tuesday between 10 AM and noon.
  • Every Thursday between 10 AM and noon.
