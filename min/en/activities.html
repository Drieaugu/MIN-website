Our activities

Which activities do you organise?
  • End of year activity with Rozenberg and Lions Club.

    Every year the Rozenbergschool organises a Saint Nicholas activity with the pupils of the 1st and 2nd grade. The students gather money by organising various actions. The gathered money is split between Ons Huis, Lichtpunt and Actie MIN. In 2019 195 childeren recieved a coupon for Dreamland.

  • Familyday

    A bi-anual event where all families are invited. We provide a warm meal and make sure that childeren are amused with various games. A warm event where you can meet other people.

  • Day of the education

    In cooperation with Huis van het kind, our parents with young childeren receive free admission to the Zilvermeer where there are plenty of amusing activities. We make sure our members get a free drink and icecream.

  • Lions familyday

    Charity organisations from our region are invited for a day filled with various folk games.

  • Leo's Club

    Two years ago our members where able to visit the Olmse Zoo. This activity included a meal and drink. It was an afternoon which everyone enjoyed. In 2019 we were able to rent a bus and take all our members to Bellwaerde. Everyone received plenty of drinks and a meal. Onze doelgroepkreeg twee jaar geleden de kans om naar de Olmse Zoo te bezoeken.
