How did MIN come about?

History

On a summer afternoon in the year of 1983 people arrived at the presbytery of Achterbos, the home of Priest Frans, with shocking news. A debt collector had just evicted a family with 5 childeren. Priest Frans believed this to be unacceptable and called upon society to help each other in case of such events. That is how MIN came about.
